## HOW TO FIND MORE LEADS

### Free Methods
1. **Google Maps** — Search "coaching institute Jaipur" / "manufacturer Jaipur" etc.
2. **JustDial** — Search by category, get phone numbers
3. **IndiaMART** — Search by product/category, find manufacturers
4. **Instagram** — Search #JaipurBusiness #JaipurBrand #JaipurFashion
5. **LinkedIn** — Search by job title (Owner, Director) + company type

### Paid Methods (When Ready)
1. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** — $99/mo, best for B2B decision maker targeting
